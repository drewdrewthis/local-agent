## Market Insights

### Pricing Patterns
- **Size correlation**: Larger properties don't always cost more proportionally
- **Location premium**: Central locations command higher rents
- **Student discounts**: University areas have competitive pricing

### Availability Timeline
- **Immediate**: Many available now
- **January 2026**: Peak availability period
- **Short-term**: Holiday periods (Dec-Jan) show sublet options

### Quality Indicators
- **Fully furnished**: Most listings
- **Renovated/modern**: Many recent renovations mentioned
- **Amenities**: Balconies, parking, proximity to transport

## Recommendations

### For Students
- Focus on Timone, Luminy, Euromed areas
- Budget: 400-550€/month
- Look for university proximity and transport links

### For Young Professionals
- Central locations (Canebière, Castellane)
- Modern renovations preferred
- Price range: 500-700€/month

### For Short-term Stays
- Check sublet sections for holiday periods
- Prices vary significantly by season/duration

### Negotiation Tips
- Lower occupancy properties may be more flexible
- Direct owner listings vs agency-managed
- Long-term commitments may offer better rates
